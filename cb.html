Chicken Biriyani:


Preparation for chicken biriyabi recipi

  1. make a few slits on all pieces and add to a large bowl.Then add the following ingredients:
    Plain Yogurt3 tbsp
    Ginger garlic paste11/4tbsp
    Biriyani masala1/2 or 1 tbsp
    Salt1/2 tsp
    Red chili powder 1/2 or 1 tsp
    Ground turmeric1/4 tsp
    Lemon juice(optional)1 tbsp
  2. Mix everything well and marinate the chicken. Cover and set this aside for 1 hour. You can also rest it overnight in the fridge.
  3. Meanwhile add 2 cups basmati rice to a large pot and rinse it at least thrice. Drain and soak in fresh water for 30 mins. Drain to a colander after 30 mins. Optional-soak a pinch of saffron strands in 2 tbsp hot milk.
  4. Heat ghee or oil in a heavy bottom pot or pressure cooker. Make sure you use a large one for this. usong whole spices is optional but recommended.you can use whatever you have.
  5. Add thinly sliced onions. On a medium heat, fry them stirring often until uniformly light brown.
  6. Caramalize the onions corrects and do not burn them as they leave a bitter taste.
  7. Add marinated chicken and saute until it becomes pale for 5 mins.
  8. Lower the flame completely. Cover and cook until the chicken is soft, tender and completely cooked.
  9. Check if the chicken is cooked by pricking with a fork or knife.It ha to be just cooked and not overdone. Evaporate any excess moisture left in the pot by cooking furthur without the lid.
  10. Taste test and add more salt if needed. Add:
    Plain yogurt1/4 cup
    Biriyani masala1 tsp
    red chili powder(optional)1/2 tsp
    Green chili pepper(optional)1 slit
    Chopped mint2 tbsp
  11. Mix everything well. spread it evenly in a single layer.
  12. Layer drained rice all over the chicken. To a separate bowl, pour 3 cups of water and add 1/4 to 1/2 tsp salt. If cooking in a pot, start with 3 cups water at the stage.Stir and taste the water. it must be slightly salty . Pour 2 cups of this across the sides of the cooker or pot. pour rest of the water over the rice gently.
  13. Level the rice gently on top. Add 2 tbsp more mint leaves. Do nt mix up everything. Optionally you can sprinkle 2 tbsp fried onions and saffron milf.
  14. Finally cover the pot or cooker.
    if cooking in a pot,cook on a medium heat until the rice is done completely . If the rice is undercooked pour 1/4 to 1/2 cup boiling water at this stage. How much to add depends on the quality of the basmati rice.Cook covered until biriyabi rice is perfectly done,grainy and fully cooked. Rest it covered for 15 mins.
    if cooking in a cooker,cook for 1 whistle.later remove the cooker from the hot burner to stop cooking further.
  15. When the pressure releases naturally, open the lid. Gently fluff up with a fork.

Serve chicken biriyni in layers - top layer with rice and bottom layer with chicken. Enjoy with raitia or shobra..

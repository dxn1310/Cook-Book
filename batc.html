Battenbury Cake

INGREDIENTS:

  • 300 gm each of butter or margarine and castor sugar
  • 6 eggs
  • 3 teaspoon baking powder
  • 300 gms of flour, sifted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  • Pink food colouring
  • 3-4 tablespoon apricot jam, warmed
  • 200-300 gms mazipan, ready-made

Directions:

  1. Line 13"x11" swiss roll tin to form three sections with 2" high sides.Cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy. Add the eggs slowly, beating well all the time.
  2. Seive the flour and baking powder together and fold into mixture along with vanilla essence. Divide the mixture into three equal portions.Colour one portion with pink colouring and another with cocoa.
  3. Fill each third of the tin with one colour. Bake at 190 degree celsius for about 20 mins or until well risen.When cold, trim the edges of the sections to an equal size, Cut each section in half lengthways and brush with jam;lay a pink next to a plain, then the brown strip and press together.
  4. Repeat to give a chequered pattern. On a sugared surface, roll out the marzipan thinly into an oblong, wrap around the jam-coated cake Pinch with thumb and forefinger along with the edges to seal. score the topin a diamond pattern and decorate with marzipan leaves.

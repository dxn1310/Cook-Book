Crispy Prawn 65

Prawn 65 is a delicious South Indian starter or appetizer. This is a spicy and crispy dry preparation in which prawns are marinated with spices and deep fried. Prawn 65 is a delicious starter recipe found in South Indian restaurants specially in Tamil Nadu. This crispy prawn fry is a perfect evening snack.

Ingredients Required:

  • Prawns / Shrimps - 500 gram
  • Red chili powder - 2 tsp
  • Lemon juice - 1 tsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Turmeric powder - ½ tsp
  • Coriander powder - 1 tsp
  • Garam masala powder - ½ tsp
  • Curry leaves -few
  • Ginger garlic paste - 1 tsp
  • Black pepper powder - ½ tsp
  • Green chilies - 2 chopped
  • Corn flour - 1tbsp
  • Rice flour - 1 tbsp

Directions:

  1. Take a mixing bowl and add cleaned prawns
  2. Add red chili powder
  3. Add coriander powder
  4. Add garam masala powder
  5. Add black pepper powder
  6. Add turmeric powder
  7. Add Salt – to taste
  8. Add ginger garlic paste
  9. Add chopped curry leaves
  10. Add chopped green chillies and mix it well
  11. Add corn flour
  12. Add rice flour and mix it well
  13. Let it marinate for one hours
  14. Drop the masala coated prawns one by one into the medium hot oil.
  15. Stir continuously and fry 5 to 8 minutes until turns golden and crisp.
  16. Drain off the prawn over a kitchen towel to remove excess oil.
  17. Serve hot with a squeeze of lemon juice.

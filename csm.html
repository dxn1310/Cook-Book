Cheesy Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients Required:

  • 25 large sized mushrooms
  • 1 cup grated processed cheese
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onions
  • 2 tsp finely chopped garlic
  • 2 tsp finely chopped green chillies
  • 1/2 tsp mustard powder
  • 1/4 cup bread crumbs
  • 2 tsp butter
  • salt to taste

Directions:

  1. Mix all the ingredients into a stuffing and keep aside.
  2. Divide the stuffing into 25 equal portions and keep aside
  3. Remove the stems of the miushrooms and discard them so as to form a cavity in the mushroom caps.
  4. Stuff each mushroom cap with a portion of the stuffing.
  5. Arrange the stufffed mushrooms on a greased baking tray and bake in a pre-heated oven at 180 oc for 10 minutes.
  6. Serve immediately.
